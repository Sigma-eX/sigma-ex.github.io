About Us

For the next great business

Sigma X is an initiative which seeks to provide quick and low-cost solutions to any problems for any individual as well as large-scale startups, communities and companies. With the rise of IT sectors, technology has shown that it is here to stay and thus, more and more people have found it necessary to work in front of a computer at one time or other. Apart from that, there has been a gargantuan rise of both small-scale and large-scale startups as it has become easier to monetise your passion and be an entrepreneur. Such fields require technical skills as well as marketing for their service/products. That is where our company comes in because we handle not only the technical aspects but everything starting from Web Development and Cyber Security to PR and Marketing. We also try and allay the complexities in understanding the new aspects in the fields of technology by providing effective workshops and one-on-one interactions.
